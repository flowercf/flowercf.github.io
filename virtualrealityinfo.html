About Virtual reality

Virtual Reality is a simulated Experience that feels real. You use a Virtual Reality Headset to enter the simulations. The simulations are designed for you to interact with them as if you're actually their. Since it's virtual reality you can enter and exit at any time you please. Nowadays technology is advancing more and more. As Virtual realirty continues to develop and become more in depth and real. However, the virtual reality headsets are usually 400$ which is kind of expensive but overall is a one of a kind experience.
